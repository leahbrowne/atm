Privacy Policy
This policy explains what information we collect when you use the Moremi Learning platform, why we collect it, and how it is used. We've written this in plain English because you deserve to understand it.
Who we are

This platform is operated by Moremi Digital Group, a UK-based growth strategy consultancy. Your access to the platform has been arranged by All Things Media, your employer. References to "we" or "the platform" in this policy refer to Moremi Digital Group.

For data protection purposes, Moremi Digital Group acts as the data processor. All Things Media acts as the data controller — meaning they are responsible for determining why your data is held and for how long.

We collect only what is necessary to provide you with a functional learning experience.

What we do not collect

We do not collect payment information. We do not access your LinkedIn connections, messages, or any data beyond your public profile fields. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. We do not sell or share your data with any third party for commercial purposes.

Who can see your data

Moremi Digital Group can see all platform usage data in order to maintain and improve the service.

Your designated All Things Media administrator can see your name, role, course progress, login frequency, and streak. They cannot see your feedback submissions.

Your colleagues can see your first name and streak on the team leaderboard only.

Clerk (our authentication provider) processes your login credentials. Their privacy policy is available at clerk.com/privacy.

How long we keep your data

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If All Things Media ends their subscription or your account is removed, your personal data will be deleted within 30 days unless you have separately requested a data export.

Your rights under GDPR

As a UK resident you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. You can exercise these rights from the Your Data section of this page, or by emailing hello@moremidigital.com. We will respond within 30 days.
